About the Pulse Rate Index

Both pages are powered by the Pulse Rate Index (PRI) — a formula that combines team strength and competitive tension to measure game quality. A game scores higher when both teams are elite programs and the result is close. Games decided by more than 8 points are excluded entirely. See the methodology sections on each page for full details.
